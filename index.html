Bharat Mata Ki Jai

Do minute ka maun un veeron ke liye, Jinhone hanste-hanste apne watan ke liye jaan de di.
Mat bhoolo unki qurbaniyan, Jinhone humein aazadi dilayi thi.
Aao milkar salaam karein un veeron ko,
Jinhone apne lahoo se hamari dharti ko sajayi thi.

15 August se pehle pahucha do is paigaam ko, Apne doston aur rishtedaron ko.
Hamesha rakhna yaad un veeron ki kahani, Jinhone is desh ke khatir ki qurbani.
Woh jinhone hanste-hanste jaan lutayi, Unki yaadon mein hai aazadi ki parchayi.
